Rohan Devraj

I am a rising 3rd-year undergraduate pursuing a B.S. in Industrial & Systems Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology, specializing in Operations Research with a minor in Computer Science (concentration in artificial intelligence/machine learning). I am passionate about optimization and data science.

Previously, I was a Summer Undergraduate Research Fellow at the Carnegie Mellon University Department of Statistics & Data Science, and I am currently interning at Optum. I am also involved in undergraduate research with the Space Systems Optimization Group, where I am advised by Prof. Koki Ho.

In addition to my academic and research work, I am proud to serve as an associate for the GT Angel Network, helping lead Georgia Tech’s first angel investment network.
